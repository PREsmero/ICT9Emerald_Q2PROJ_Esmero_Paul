There are many beautiful places in the Philippines, its no suprise that some people get confused on where to go for their vacation. Well have no fear, today were here to recommend you some of the most beautiful, mind-blowing, stunning, and majestic vacation places you can visit!

BORACAY


Boracay is a resort island, being one of the best places for relaxation and stress relief, its known for its beaches, a really amazing view of the sunset, and clear and clean waters.

Chocolate Hills


Located in Bohol, chocolate hills is a very beautiful sight. The unique thing about these hills is that when its dry season, the grass would have a color of brown, thus the name chocolate hills.

Siaragao


Siaragao, aka the "Surfing Capital of the Phillipines" is an island consisting of nine municiplaities in Surigao del Norte's province. There are alot of activities that you can do, cave explorations, rock climbing, and of course the surfing.
